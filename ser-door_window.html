Doors and Windows

At Ternak Home Improvement Inc., our doors and windows services enhance your home’s beauty, security, and energy efficiency. From stylish entry doors to modern, energy-saving windows, our expert team delivers custom solutions that elevate your home’s functionality and curb appeal.

Our Doors and Windows Services

1. Entry and Patio Doors
Choose from elegant front doors, sliding glass doors, or French patio doors to create a welcoming entrance or seamless indoor-outdoor flow.

2. Window Replacement and Installation
We install energy-efficient windows, including double-hung, casement, bay, or custom designs, to enhance natural light and ventilation.

3. Storm and Security Doors
Protect your home from the elements and enhance security with sturdy storm doors or reinforced security doors tailored to your needs.

4. Window and Door Repairs
We repair or replace damaged frames, glass, seals, and hardware to restore functionality and improve energy efficiency.

5. Custom Window Treatments
Complement your new windows with custom blinds, shutters, or shades for added style, privacy, and light control.
