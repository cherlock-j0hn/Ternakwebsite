Basement and Waterproofing

At Ternak Home Improvement Inc., we specialize in transforming your basement into a dry, functional, and inviting space. Our expert basement and waterproofing services ensure your home remains protected from water damage, mold, and structural issues, providing peace of mind and increasing your property’s value.

Our Basement and Waterproofing Services

1. Interior Waterproofing
We install interior drainage systems, sump pumps, and vapor barriers to manage water seepage and prevent flooding. Our solutions are designed to redirect water away from your home efficiently.

2. Exterior Waterproofing
Our team applies waterproof coatings, installs exterior drainage systems, and seals foundation cracks to stop water from entering your basement at its source.

3. Foundation Crack Repair
Using industry-leading materials, we repair cracks in your foundation to prevent leaks and ensure structural integrity.

4. Sump Pump Installation and Maintenance
We install reliable sump pumps to remove excess water and offer maintenance services to keep them functioning optimally.

5. Basement Finishing and Remodeling
Once your basement is dry, we can transform it into a livable space, such as a home office, gym, or entertainment area, customized to your needs.
