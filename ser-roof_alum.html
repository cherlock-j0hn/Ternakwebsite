At Ternak Home Improvement Inc., our roofing and aluminum services provide durable, high-quality solutions to protect and enhance your home’s exterior. Our expert team delivers customized roofing and aluminum installations, combining skilled craftsmanship with premium materials to ensure long-lasting performance and aesthetic appeal.

Our Roofing and Aluminum Services

1. Roof Installation
We install a variety of roofing materials, including asphalt shingles, metal, and tile, designed to withstand harsh weather and complement your home’s style.

2. Aluminum Roofing and Siding
Our team installs durable, low-maintenance aluminum roofing and siding, offering superior weather resistance and a sleek, modern look.

3. Roof Repairs and Maintenance
We fix leaks, replace damaged shingles, and perform routine maintenance to extend the life of your roof and prevent costly issues.

4. Aluminum Trim and Soffits
We install aluminum trim, soffits, and fascia to enhance your home’s exterior, improve ventilation, and protect against moisture damage.

5. Roof Inspections and Upgrades
Our experts conduct thorough roof inspections and offer upgrades like energy-efficient coatings or skylights to improve functionality and curb appeal.
