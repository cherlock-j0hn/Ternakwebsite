Fences and Decks

At Ternak Home Improvement Inc., our fences and decks services transform your outdoor space into a beautiful, functional, and private oasis. Our expert team delivers custom designs and high-quality craftsmanship to create durable, stylish fences and decks that enhance your home’s curb appeal and usability.

Our Fences and Decks Services

1. Custom Fence Installation
We design and install fences in wood, vinyl, aluminum, or composite, tailored for privacy, security, or decorative purposes to suit your property.

2. Deck Design and Construction
Create your dream outdoor living space with custom decks built from premium wood or low-maintenance composite materials, designed for durability and style.

3. Fence Repairs and Maintenance
We repair damaged fences, replace broken panels, and provide staining or sealing to extend the life and appearance of your fence.

4. Deck Repairs and Refinishing
Our team restores worn decks through repairs, refinishing, or staining, ensuring safety and a refreshed look.

5. Custom Features
Enhance your fence or deck with additions like gates, pergolas, built-in seating, or lighting for added functionality and aesthetic appeal.
