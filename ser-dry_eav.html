Drywall and Eavestrough

At Ternak Home Improvement Inc., our drywall and eavestrough services deliver seamless interior finishes and reliable exterior water management to enhance your home’s appearance, durability, and protection. Our expert team provides customized solutions to meet your specific needs, ensuring high-quality results that elevate your property.

Our Drywall and Eavestrough Services

1. Drywall Installation
We install high-quality drywall for walls and ceilings, creating smooth, durable surfaces ready for painting or texturing, perfect for new construction or renovations.

2. Drywall Repair and Finishing
From patching holes to fixing cracks and water damage, we restore your drywall to a flawless finish with expert taping, mudding, and sanding.

3. Eavestrough Installation
We install seamless aluminum or steel eavestroughs to efficiently channel rainwater away from your home, protecting your foundation and landscaping.

4. Eavestrough Cleaning and Repairs
Our team provides thorough cleaning and maintenance to prevent clogs, as well as repairs for leaks, sagging, or damaged eavestroughs.

5. Custom Drywall Features
Elevate your interiors with custom drywall designs, such as accent walls, arched doorways, or built-in shelving, tailored to your style.
