Concrete and Porches

At Ternak Home Improvement Inc., our concrete and porches services are designed to enhance your home’s functionality, curb appeal, and durability. From sturdy concrete foundations to inviting porch designs, our expert team delivers custom solutions that elevate your outdoor and structural space.

Our Concrete and Porches Services

1. Concrete Foundations and Slabs
We pour and finish strong, level concrete foundations and slabs for homes, garages, or additions, ensuring structural integrity and longevity.

2. Custom Porch Design and Construction
Create a welcoming front porch, cozy back porch, or spacious wraparound porch with custom designs that blend style and functionality.

3. Concrete Patios and Walkways
Transform your outdoor space with durable, stylish concrete patios and walkways, available in stamped, stained, or polished finishes.

4. Concrete Repair and Resurfacing
We repair cracked or uneven concrete surfaces and offer resurfacing solutions to restore their appearance and strength.

5. Porch Repairs and Upgrades
From replacing worn decking to adding railings or screening, we restore and enhance existing porches to improve safety and aesthetics.
