Flooring and Paving

At Ternak Home Improvement Inc., our flooring and paving services elevate your home’s interior and exterior with stunning, durable surfaces. Our skilled team delivers customized solutions, combining expert craftsmanship with high-quality materials to create floors and paved areas that enhance both aesthetics and functionality.

Our Flooring and Paving Services

1. Hardwood and Laminate Flooring
We install and refinish hardwood or laminate floors, offering a range of styles and finishes to add warmth and elegance to your interiors.

2. Tile and Vinyl Flooring
Our team installs durable, stylish tile or vinyl flooring for kitchens, bathrooms, or other spaces, designed for easy maintenance and lasting beauty.

3. Paving for Driveways and Walkways
We create stunning driveways, walkways, and patios using concrete, pavers, or stone, tailored to complement your home’s exterior.

4. Flooring Repairs and Restoration
We repair damaged floors, replace worn sections, and restore existing flooring to revitalize its appearance and functionality.

5. Paving Repairs and Resurfacing
Our services include fixing cracked or uneven paving, resealing surfaces, and refreshing outdoor areas to ensure durability and curb appeal.
