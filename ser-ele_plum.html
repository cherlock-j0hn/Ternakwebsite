Electric and Plumbing

At Ternak Home Improvement Inc., our electrical and plumbing services ensure your home operates safely, efficiently, and reliably. Our licensed professionals deliver customized solutions to meet your needs, enhancing your home’s functionality and comfort with expert craftsmanship.

Our Electrical and Plumbing Services

1. Electrical Installations
We install wiring, lighting, outlets, and circuit panels for new constructions, renovations, or upgrades, ensuring compliance with safety codes.

2. Plumbing Installations
From pipes to fixtures, we install water heaters, faucets, sinks, and more, designed for durability and efficiency.

3. Electrical Repairs and Upgrades
We troubleshoot and fix electrical issues, upgrade outdated systems, and install energy-efficient lighting or smart home solutions.

4. Plumbing Repairs and Maintenance
Our team handles leaks, clogs, and pipe repairs, along with routine maintenance to keep your plumbing system running smoothly.

5. Emergency Services
We offer prompt electrical and plumbing repairs for urgent issues like power outages or burst pipes, ensuring your home stays safe and functional.
